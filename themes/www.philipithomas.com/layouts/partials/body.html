Philip I. Thomas

I live in NYC, where I am building Moonlight - a professional community for software developers. I graduated from WashU in 2013, where I studied systems engineering and physics. From 2013 to 2015, I developed engineering systems at OpenDNS, which Cisco bought. In 2015, I completed a fellowship at Y Combinator. From 2015 to 2017, I was the founder of Staffjoy, a scheduling startup that shut down and open-sourced its code. From 2017 to 2019, I traveled the world full-time while living out of a suitcase. I have a fluffy dog named Queso, maintain a strict ketogenic diet, and tinker with fountain pens.
